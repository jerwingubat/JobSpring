Job Description

TechSolutions Inc. is looking for an experienced Senior Frontend Developer to join our growing team. You'll be responsible for building responsive, accessible user interfaces for our SaaS platform while working closely with our design and backend teams.

Responsibilities:

  • Develop new user-facing features using React.js
  • Build reusable components and front-end libraries for future use
  • Translate designs and wireframes into high-quality code
  • Optimize components for maximum performance across devices and browsers
  • Collaborate with product designers and backend engineers
  • Participate in code reviews and mentor junior developers
  • Stay up-to-date with emerging frontend technologies

Requirements:

  • 5+ years of frontend development experience
  • Strong proficiency in JavaScript, including ES6+ syntax
  • 3+ years of experience with React.js and its core principles
  • Experience with popular React workflows (Redux, Context API, etc.)
  • Familiarity with RESTful APIs and modern authorization mechanisms
  • Knowledge of modern frontend build pipelines and tools
  • Experience with common frontend development tools such as Babel, Webpack, NPM, etc.
  • Familiarity with code versioning tools (Git)
  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or related field, or equivalent experience

Nice to Have:

  • Experience with TypeScript
  • Familiarity with server-side rendering
  • Knowledge of testing frameworks (Jest, React Testing Library)
  • Experience with CI/CD pipelines
  • Contributions to open-source projects

About TechSolutions Inc.

TechSolutions is a fast-growing SaaS company providing innovative solutions to the logistics industry. Founded in 2015, we've grown to serve over 1,000 customers worldwide with our cutting-edge platform.

We offer a collaborative, remote-first work environment with competitive compensation and benefits. Our team is passionate about building great products and fostering professional growth.
