Our Story

Who We Are

TalentFinders was founded in 2010 with a simple mission: to connect great people with great companies. What began as a small recruitment agency in New York has grown into a nationwide network of recruitment specialists serving clients across industries.

Our team brings together decades of combined experience in talent acquisition, human resources, and career counseling. We're proud to have helped thousands of candidates find fulfilling careers and supported hundreds of companies in building exceptional teams.

Unlike larger, impersonal agencies, we maintain a boutique approach that allows us to truly understand both our candidates and clients. Our recruiters become trusted partners in your career or hiring journey.
